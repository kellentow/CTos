Key Features of CTos

  • Scalability: Easily expand your network by adding more Proxy Hubs.
  • Reliability: Ensure continuous connectivity with robust failover mechanisms.
  • Security: Protect your data with advanced encryption and secure protocols.
  • Efficiency: Optimize data routing and reduce latency across your network.

Additional Features

  • Centralized Management: Control and monitor your network from a centralized Main Hub.
  • Flexibility: Connect clients directly to the Main Hub or through scalable Proxy Hubs.
  • Customization: Configure CTos to meet the specific needs of your network infrastructure.
