What is CTos?

CTos is an interconnected system designed to simplify and enhance the management of your internet infrastructure. It offers a robust network of Proxy Hubs and Main Hubs that seamlessly connect clients and provide efficient data routing.

How It Works

The CTos system operates through a series of interconnected hubs:

  • Client: Connects to the network via a Proxy Hub or directly to the Main Hub.
  • Proxy Hub: Can connect to another Proxy Hub or directly to the Main Hub, ensuring flexible and scalable network architecture.
  • Main Hub: Centralized hub that manages data flow and connectivity across the entire network.

Key Features

  • Scalability: Easily expand your network by adding more Proxy Hubs.
  • Reliability: Ensure continuous connectivity with robust failover mechanisms.
  • Security: Protect your data with advanced encryption and secure protocols.
  • Efficiency: Optimize data routing and reduce latency across your network.

Getting Started

Ready to revolutionize your internet infrastructure with CTos? Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Set up your Main Hub.
  2. Configure your Proxy Hubs.
  3. Connect your clients to the nearest Proxy Hub or directly to the Main Hub.
  4. Monitor and manage your network using the CTos dashboard.
